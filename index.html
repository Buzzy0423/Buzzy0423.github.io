Selected Work

Visuo-Tactile Robotic Manipulation

Integrating tactile sensing into robotic manipulation at the SLURM Lab, USC, turning raw touch signals into feedback that helps robots grasp and adapt in the real world.

Tactile Sensing Robotic Manipulation PyTorch
Repo Demo

Uncertainty-Aware Medical Image Enhancement

A dual-branch model that enhances medical images while estimating pixel-wise uncertainty, using a Jeffreys prior loss to jointly optimize both. Undergraduate thesis at SUSTech.

Medical Image Quality Assessment

A CLIP-based quality assessment algorithm with adapters and multi-task learning, ranked first on five benchmark datasets against state-of-the-art methods.

Diffusion-based Image Restoration

A diffusion-based restoration method tackling semantic consistency, with custom preprocessing and latent noise strategies. Developed during an internship at HiDream, Beijing.

About

I'm a CS master's student at USC researching visuo-tactile perception for robotic manipulation at the SLURM Lab. I want robots to feel what they touch and use that signal to grasp, adjust, and adapt in the real world.

Before USC, I studied at SUSTech, where I published work on medical image enhancement, domain adaptation, and quality assessment. I also interned at HiDream, developing diffusion-based image restoration methods.
