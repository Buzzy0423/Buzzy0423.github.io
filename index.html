About

I'm a CS master's student at USC, working on tactile sensing for robotic manipulation. I want robots to feel what they touch — and use that feedback to manipulate objects with real dexterity, not just replayed motions.

Before USC, I studied at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), where I worked on medical image enhancement and domain adaptation.
